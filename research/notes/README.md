# research/notes/

Raw notes, transcripts, screenshots, and external research from every session.

## Filename convention
`YYYY-MM-DD-<slug>.md`

Examples:
- `2026-05-19-mystic-launch-wedge-observations.md`
- `2026-05-20-kalshi-orderbook-anomaly.md`

## Template (paste at top of new note)
```
# <Short Title>

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM ET
**Session:** <agent name / human>
**Tags:** mystic | kxbtc15m | btc-hourly | weather | sports | ops | accounting | other
**Source:** <chat link, URL, paper, file, screenshot path>

## Raw
<Paste raw content here. Do not edit for prose - this is the source artifact.>

## Observations
<Your reading of it.>

## Implications
<What this might mean for ZeroTrading.>

## Next
<Concrete next step, or `none`.>
```

## Rules
- Append-only. Do not delete or rewrite past notes - supersede them with a new dated note.
- No secrets, API keys, or account identifiers.
- Link out instead of pasting copyrighted material at length.
